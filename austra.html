Australia

Overview

Australia, the Land Down Under, is a vast and diverse country known for its unique wildlife, stunning landscapes, and vibrant cities. With a population of around 25 million people, Australia is characterized by its multicultural society, where people from various backgrounds and cultures coexist harmoniously. The capital city, Canberra, is known for its modern architecture and cultural institutions. The Australian Dollar (AUD) is the official currency. Australia boasts a wide range of climates, from tropical in the north to temperate in the south, offering diverse experiences for travelers.
