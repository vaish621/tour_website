United States

Overview

The United States of America, commonly known as the USA or America, is a diverse and vast country located in North America. It is known for its cultural melting pot, iconic landmarks, and varied landscapes, ranging from towering mountains to vast plains and stunning coastlines. Washington, D.C., is the capital city, and English is the primary language spoken. The currency is the United States Dollar (USD). The USA is home to numerous world-famous attractions, including the Grand Canyon, Statue of Liberty, and Yellowstone National Park. With a rich history and diverse population, the USA offers a wide range of experiences, from exploring vibrant cities to enjoying the natural beauty of its national parks.
