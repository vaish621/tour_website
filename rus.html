Russia

Overview

Russia, the largest country in the world, spans Eastern Europe and northern Asia, covering more than one-eighth of the Earth's inhabited land area. Known for its rich history, diverse culture, and iconic landmarks, Russia is a fascinating destination. Moscow, the capital city, is home to the Kremlin and Red Square, while St. Petersburg boasts the State Hermitage Museum and the Winter Palace. The official language is Russian, and the currency is the Russian Ruble (RUB). Russia experiences a varied climate, from arctic conditions in the north to a humid subtropical climate in the south, offering different experiences across its regions.
